What We Offer

Our Dental Services

From preventive care to complete smile transformations, we provide comprehensive dental treatments in a comfortable, modern environment.

  • General Dentistry

    Routine exams, X-rays, professional cleaning and cavity fillings to keep your teeth healthy and strong year after year.

  • Teeth Whitening

    Professional-grade in-office whitening that removes years of staining in a single visit — safe, fast and dramatically effective.

  • Dental Implants

    Permanent, natural-looking replacements for missing teeth. Titanium implants that look, feel and function just like your real teeth.

  • Orthodontics

    Traditional braces and clear-aligner therapy to straighten teeth and correct bite issues for patients of all ages.

  • Porcelain Veneers

    Ultra-thin ceramic shells bonded to the front surface of teeth to instantly correct shape, size, colour and minor misalignment.

  • Root Canal Therapy

    Gentle, pain-free treatment to save an infected tooth, eliminate discomfort and restore full function — often in a single appointment.

Meet the Doctor

Dr. Yousef Abu Koush

Dr. Yousef Abu Koush is a highly experienced general and cosmetic dentist with over 20 years of dedicated practice. He earned his dental degree with distinction and completed advanced training in implantology and aesthetic dentistry at leading European institutions.

His philosophy is simple: every patient deserves pain-free, honest, and personalised care. Whether you are coming in for a routine clean or a full smile makeover, Dr. Abu Koush takes the time to listen, explain, and deliver results that last.

  • Board-Certified General & Cosmetic Dentist
  • Fellow of the International College of Dentists
  • Advanced Training in Dental Implants & Veneers
  • Fluent in Arabic, English & Hebrew
